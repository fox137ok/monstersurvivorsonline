About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed roguelike survival game where you must battle against endless waves of monsters. As time progresses, the challenges become increasingly difficult, testing your skills and strategic thinking.

In this thrilling adventure, you'll collect power-ups, unlock new abilities, and discover unique character combinations that can help you survive longer against the monster hordes. Every run is different, offering endless replayability and excitement.

With its simple controls but deep gameplay mechanics, Monster Survivors is easy to learn but difficult to master, making it perfect for both casual gamers and hardcore enthusiasts looking for a challenge.

Tips & Strategies

  • Keep Moving: Standing still is a death sentence. Constantly move to avoid being surrounded by monsters.
  • Focus on Synergies: Some weapons and abilities work better together. Experiment with different combinations to find powerful synergies.
  • Prioritize Experience: Collect experience gems to level up quickly and gain access to more powerful abilities.
  • Manage Your Space: Try to keep monsters grouped together to maximize the effectiveness of area attacks.
  • Plan Your Upgrades: Think carefully about which upgrades to choose when leveling up. A balanced build is often better than focusing on a single aspect.
